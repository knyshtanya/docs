FAQ

  • How do I add a new word? You can add words manually, by voice, or from a photo.
  • How does repetition work? Wordlio shows words again based on how difficult they were for you.
  • Can I import my own word list? Yes, you can import your own list from a file.
  • Does Wordlio work offline? Some features may require an internet connection.
