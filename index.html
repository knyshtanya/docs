FAQ

How do I add a new word?

You can add words manually, by voice, from a photo, or import your own list from a file.

How does repetition work?

You rate each word after reviewing it. Wordlio uses this to decide when to show it again.

Does Wordlio work offline?

Yes. Your words and progress are stored on your device and available offline.

Can I import my own word list?

Yes. You can import your own words from a file and use them right away.

Will I lose my words?

No. All your words are stored locally on your device.
