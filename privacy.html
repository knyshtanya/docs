Wordlio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Wordlio does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contacts.

The app stores the data you create:

  • words you add
  • translations
  • examples and notes

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2. Microphone Access

Wordlio may request access to your device’s microphone to allow voice input when adding words.

Audio is processed using Apple’s speech recognition services and is not stored by the app.

3. Notifications

Wordlio may send local notifications (reminders) to help you review words.

These notifications are scheduled on your device and do not involve external servers.

4. File Import

If you import word lists from files, the data is processed locally on your device.

No files are uploaded or shared.

5. Third-Party Services

Wordlio does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple system frameworks.

The app does not transmit your data to external servers.

7. Children’s Privacy

Wordlio does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
